城巴將於 15/12 至 1/1/2019 期間提供 NA10 特別線服務。由是次服務期開始，此特別線延長至港珠澳大橋香港口岸。NA10 鴨脷洲開 04:55，回程港珠澳大橋開 01:05，巴士將在機場等候至 01:15 原訂開出時間。

Citybus will provide special service Rt. NA10 between 15/12 and 1/1/2019. Note that NA10 is extended to HZMB Hong Kong Port from this service period onwards. NA10 will depart from Ap Lei Chau at 04:55, return journey from HZMB port departs at 01:05 (after arriving Airport, the bus will wait until 01:15).
