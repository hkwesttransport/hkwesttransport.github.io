由 26/2/2018 起，新巴 23B 增設與新巴 25 線的免費轉乘優惠，方便乘客來往半山區及天后廟道。
現時新巴 23 及城巴 12M 線已設有與新巴 25 線的轉乘優惠。

From 26/2/2018 onwards, NWFB No 23B will add a free bus-bus interchange with NWFB 25 to facilitate passengers travelling between Mid-Levels & Tin Hau Temple Rd.
CTB 12M & NWFB 23 are already having free bus-bus interchange with NWFB 25.
