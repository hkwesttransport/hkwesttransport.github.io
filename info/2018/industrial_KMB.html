據商業電台報導，今晚有部份九巴司機將於八時起罷駛半小時，總站開出班次或會延誤，而正在行駛中的班次亦可能會於中途停車半小時。是次行動將不會影響新巴城巴班次。

According to Commercial Radio, some KMB bus captains are having industrial action tonight at 8pm for half an hour. During industrial action, there may be no KMB departing from various termini, and KMB buses on their journey may also stop for half an hour. No affect to NWFB/CTB departures.
